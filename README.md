## 第一章: 计算科学的历史和哲学基础

Chapter 1: the History and Philosophy Base of Computing Science 

在不同尺度下观测, 宇宙呈现出不同的图景和动态。

When observed at different scales, the universe displays different landscape and dynamics.

我们不加说明地给出下列定义: 

We claim some definitions without further explanation:

数学：研究“类人意识”能在意识内部创造的抽象结构.

Math: study on the abstract structures which can be created inside human-like consciousness.

物理: 研究“类人意识”能在意识外部观测到的具体结构.

Physics: study on the concrete structures which can be observed by human-like consciousness.

计算科学: 研究抽象和具体结构的尺度放缩与优化.

Computing Science: study on scale zooming and optimization of abstract & concrete structures.

尺度放缩, 是在包含不同尺度的楼梯上，爬上爬下。

By scale zooming, we mean walk up and down on the staircase of scales.

优化，指的是算力的高效使用，尤其是如何使用较少的算力来连接不同的尺度。

By optimization, we mean efficient application of computing powers, especially using less computing power to connect different scales.
