## 序言 计算科学身处的宇宙

Preface: the universe where computing science lives

我们是宇宙了解自身的方式. 卡尔萨根.

We are a way for the universe to know itself.  *Karl Sagan*

在不同尺度下观测, 宇宙呈现出不同的图景和动态。

When observed at different scales, the universe displays different landscape and dynamics.

我们不加说明地给出下列定义: 

We claim some definitions without further explanation:

数学：研究“类人意识”能在意识内部创造的抽象结构.

Math: study on the abstract structures which can be created inside human-like consciousness.

物理: 研究“类人意识”能在意识外部观测到的具体结构.

Physics: study on the concrete structures which can be observed by human-like consciousness.

计算科学: 研究数学和物理结构的尺度放缩与优化.

Computing Science: study on scale zooming and optimization of math & physics structures.

尺度放缩, 是在包含不同尺度的楼梯上，爬上爬下。

By scale zooming, we mean walk up and down on the staircase of scales.

优化，指的是算力的高效使用，尤其是如何使用较少的算力来连接不同的尺度。

By optimization, we mean efficient application of computing powers, especially using less computing power to connect different scales.

人类开始计算, 早在基于电子技术的离散计算机诞生之前。

Human started computing, long before the birth of discrete computers based on electronics, 

更基础的假设是, 所有的动物、植物，在其身体内，都完成了隐式的计算。

More basic assumption: all animals and plants do implicit computing in their body.

进化，就是通过遗传算法对碳基计算机进行了优化。

Evolution is an optimization to the carbon computers with genetic algorithm. 

大脑计算效率最佳的演化结果，就是智人。而意识，不过是一种非凡而壮丽的算法。

The best case of "brain computing efficiency", is homo sapiens. And consciousness is nothing more than an extraordinary and splendid algorithm.
