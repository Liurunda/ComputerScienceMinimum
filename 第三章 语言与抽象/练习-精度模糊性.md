# 练习 3 — 精度-模糊性光谱

Exercise 3 — The Precision-Ambiguity Spectrum

---

**(a) 定位四个领域**

**(a) Locate four domains**

本章讨论了四个领域对语言精度的不同需求：医学（极高精度）、编程语言（高精度）、法律/外交（中等精度，保留妥协空间）、诗歌/电影（低精度，追求联想空间）。在"精度-模糊性"轴上为这四个领域标注位置，并各用一句话解释你如此定位的原因。

This chapter discusses four domains with different requirements for language precision: medicine (extreme precision), programming languages (high precision), law/diplomacy (moderate precision, preserving space for compromise), poetry/film (low precision, pursuing space for association). Mark the positions of these four domains on a "precision-ambiguity" axis, and explain your placement in one sentence each.

---

**(b) 寻找一个"中间地带"的例子**

**(b) Find a "middle ground" example**

找一个不属于以上四类、但在精度-模糊性上有独特取舍的领域（如食谱、乐谱、体育战术描述、游戏攻略等）。分析：这个领域的语言在哪些维度上精确？在哪些维度上刻意模糊？这些取舍是否合理？

Find a domain not among the four listed above, but one that makes a unique tradeoff on precision versus ambiguity (e.g., recipes, musical scores, sports tactics, game guides, etc.). Analyze: on which dimensions is this domain's language precise? On which dimensions is it deliberately vague? Are these tradeoffs justified?

---

**(c) 模糊性的正向价值**

**(c) The positive value of ambiguity**

本章声称"模糊性是联想、创造和幽默的来源，也是社会能妥协运转下去的前提"。选以下一个角度，用具体例子论证或反驳：

The chapter claims that "ambiguity is a source of association, creativity, and humor, and the precondition for society to compromise and function." Choose one of the following angles and defend or refute with concrete examples:

- 一个科学发现（或技术进步）的诞生，依赖于某个模糊概念的"可联想空间"
- 一个政治或社会妥协的成功，恰恰因为协议文本在关键处保持了模糊
- 一个笑话或文学作品的趣味，一旦被精确解释就消失了

A scientific discovery (or technological advance) whose birth depended on the "associative space" of a vague concept; A political or social compromise that succeeded precisely because the agreement text remained ambiguous at key points; A joke or literary work whose humor/charm disappears the moment it is explained precisely.
