## 比特与熵

Bits and Entropy

### 信息的物理性

信息不是漂浮在物理世界之上的幽灵。

Information is not a ghost floating above the physical world.

当第一个智人在兽骨上刻下划痕时，他不仅创造了一个比特——他更将一个物理事实转变为了另一个物理事实：碳粉从骨槽中剥离，沟槽与平面的光学反射率产生差异，光子携带这个差异进入视网膜，化学电位沿视神经传导。信息从始至终都在物理基底上运行。

When the first Homo sapiens carved a notch on a bone, he not only created a bit — he transformed one physical fact into another: carbon powder was stripped from the groove, the optical reflectivity between the groove and the flat surface differed, photons carried this difference into the retina, and chemical potentials traveled along the optic nerve. Information ran on a physical substrate from beginning to end.

**信息，是有结构的物质在时空中产生的可观测差异。**

**Information is the observable difference produced by structured matter in spacetime.**

这一章的任务是：从最底层开始，建立信息与物理世界之间的尺度对应关系——而这个对应关系的数学核心，是熵。

The task of this chapter: starting from the very bottom, to establish the scale correspondence between information and the physical world — and the mathematical core of this correspondence is entropy.

### 热力学熵：无知的数量化

Thermodynamic Entropy: the quantification of ignorance

19世纪，物理学家遇到了一个尴尬的问题：他们知道蒸汽机的能量守恒（热力学第一定律），但不知道为什么能量总是从热的物体流向冷的物体，而不是反过来。

In the 19th century, physicists encountered an embarrassing problem: they knew energy was conserved in steam engines (the First Law of Thermodynamics), but they did not know why energy always flowed from hot objects to cold ones, and never the reverse.

牛顿力学方程在时间反演下是对称的——把 t 换成 -t，方程依然成立。但一杯热水放在房间里从来不会自动变得更热。这个不对称性去哪里找？

Newtonian mechanics is symmetric under time reversal — replace t with -t, and the equations still hold. Yet a cup of hot water placed in a room never spontaneously gets hotter. Where does this asymmetry come from?

玻尔兹曼的答案是：你没有看到反向过程，不是因为物理定律禁止它，而是因为它的概率低到不可思议。

Boltzmann's answer: you don't see the reverse process not because physical laws forbid it, but because its probability is unimaginably low.

一杯热水中的分子有大约 10^23 个。一个"宏观状态"（比如水温60°C）对应着天文数字的"微观状态"（每个分子的具体位置和速度）。在这所有可能的微观状态中，分子能量均匀分布的"热平衡"状态占据了压倒性的绝大多数。系统自然地走向平衡，不是因为有某个力在推它——而是因为平衡态在状态空间中统治了概率。

A cup of hot water has about 10^23 molecules. One "macrostate" (e.g., water at 60°C) corresponds to an astronomical number of "microstates" (the specific position and velocity of each molecule). Among all possible microstates, the thermal equilibrium states — where molecular energies are evenly spread — occupy the overwhelming majority. The system naturally drifts toward equilibrium not because some force pushes it — but because equilibrium states dominate the probability in state space.

玻尔兹曼定义：

Boltzmann defined:

$$S = k \ln W$$

其中 S 是熵，k 是玻尔兹曼常数，W 是该宏观状态下可能的微观状态数。

Where S is entropy, k is Boltzmann's constant, and W is the number of possible microstates of the given macrostate.

换句话说：**熵，是对"我们不知道什么"的精确度量。** 当你测量到水温是60°C，你知道了宏观状态；但你对分子的具体排布一无所知。那个"无知"的规模——取对数之后——就是热力学熵。

In other words: **entropy is a precise measure of "what we don't know."** When you measure the water temperature to be 60°C, you know the macrostate; but you know nothing about the specific arrangement of molecules. That scale of "ignorance" — after taking the logarithm — is thermodynamic entropy.

### 信息熵：惊讶的数学

Information Entropy: the mathematics of surprise

1948年，香农在贝尔实验室思考一个完全不同的问题：如何度量一条消息中的信息量？

In 1948, Shannon at Bell Labs was thinking about a completely different question: how to measure the amount of information in a message?

他给出了一个看似荒谬的答案：**一条消息的信息量，等于它消除的不确定性。** 而"不确定性"的数学结构——令所有人震惊的是——与玻尔兹曼的热力学熵在形式上完全同构。

He gave a seemingly absurd answer: **the information content of a message equals the uncertainty it eliminates.** And the mathematical structure of "uncertainty" — to everyone's astonishment — was formally isomorphic to Boltzmann's thermodynamic entropy.

香农定义：

Shannon defined:

$$H(X) = -\sum_{i} p(x_i) \log_2 p(x_i)$$

如果有一个公平硬币（正反各50%），抛一次的结果携带的H = 1比特的信息。如果硬币被做了手脚，99%概率正面，则H ≈ 0.08比特——因为你已经几乎知道结果，消息到来时的"惊讶"很小。

For a fair coin (50% each side), one coin toss carries H = 1 bit of information. If the coin is rigged to land heads 99% of the time, then H ≈ 0.08 bits — because you almost already know the outcome; the "surprise" when the message arrives is small.

这不就是玻尔兹曼的 S = k ln W 吗？W 是微观状态数，而香农的 -\sum p log p 是在问：这个概率分布下，你"不知道"的程度有多大？

Is this not Boltzmann's S = k ln W? W is the number of microstates, while Shannon's -\sum p log p asks: under this probability distribution, how much do you "not know"?

当所有微观状态等概率时（p = 1/W），香农熵退化为：

When all microstates are equally probable (p = 1/W), Shannon entropy reduces to:

$$H = -\sum_{i=1}^{W} \frac{1}{W} \log \frac{1}{W} = \log W$$

这就是玻尔兹曼熵，只差一个常数因子 k（以及对数的底数）。

This is Boltzmann's entropy, differing only by a constant factor k (and the base of the logarithm).

**热力学熵和信息熵，是同一个数学结构在两种尺度下的表达：一个在原子尺度上度量物理秩序，一个在符号尺度上度量语义秩序。**

**Thermodynamic entropy and information entropy are the same mathematical structure expressed at two different scales: one measures physical order at the atomic scale, the other measures semantic order at the symbolic scale.**

### 麦克斯韦妖与兰道尔界限

Maxwell's Demon and Landauer's Bound

麦克斯韦在1871年提出了一个思想实验：一个"妖精"站在一个隔热的容器中间，容器被一扇小门隔开。妖精测量每个飞来的分子速度，快的放右边，慢的放左边。于是左边变冷，右边变热——熵减少了，而妖精没有做任何"功"。这不就违反了热力学第二定律吗？

In 1871, Maxwell proposed a thought experiment: a "demon" stands in the middle of an insulated container divided by a small door. The demon measures the speed of each approaching molecule, letting fast ones go right, slow ones go left. Thus the left cools and the right heats — entropy decreases, yet the demon does no "work." Wouldn't this violate the Second Law?

这个问题困惑了物理学界近一个世纪。直到1982年，兰道尔（Rolf Landauer）和本内特（Charles Bennett）才彻底解决了它。

This problem puzzled physicists for nearly a century. It was not until 1982 that Landauer and Bennett fully resolved it.

关键在于：**妖精所做的"测量"，以及为决定开关门方向所执行的"计算"，本身就是一个物理过程。** 妖精必须将分子速度的信息存储在大脑中（或控制门的机关里）。当存储容量满时，它必须擦除旧的记录才能写入新的。

The key: **the "measurement" the demon performs, and the "computation" it executes to decide which way to open the door, are themselves physical processes.** The demon must store the molecular velocity information in its brain (or in the mechanism controlling the door). When its storage fills up, it must erase old records to write new ones.

而擦除信息——根据兰道尔原理——**至少消耗 kT ln 2 的能量，并向环境排放至少同样数量的热熵。**

And erasing information — according to Landauer's Principle — **costs at minimum kT ln 2 of energy, and dumps at least that much thermal entropy into the environment.**

擦除一个比特，宇宙的物理熵至少增加 k ln 2。

Erasing one bit increases the universe's physical entropy by at least k ln 2.

信息不是免费的。信息是物理的。麦克斯韦妖没有推翻热力学第二定律——它只是把这个定律从蒸汽机扩展到了计算。

Information is not free. Information is physical. Maxwell's demon did not overthrow the Second Law of Thermodynamics — it merely extended that law from steam engines to computation.

这就是连接计算科学与物理世界的那个"常数"：1 比特 ↔ k ln 2 焦耳/开尔文。这是比特与分子之间的尺度换算表。

This is the "constant" that connects computing science to the physical world: 1 bit ↔ k ln 2 joules/kelvin. This is the scale conversion table between bits and molecules.

### 熵作为"尺度放缩"的度量

Entropy as a Measure of "Scale Zooming"

现在我们可以精确地理解，序言所说的"计算科学是关于尺度放缩与优化"到底在优化什么。

Now we can understand precisely what the preface meant when it said "computing science is about scale zooming and optimization" — what exactly is being optimized.

计算，本质上是在两个方向上的熵转换：

Computing is, in essence, entropy conversion in two directions:

**向上（压缩／抽象／降维）**：从高维的、充满冗余的原始信号中，提取出低维的、紧凑的结构表示。比如将一部电影（GB级的像素阵列）压缩成一句话的梗概（几十个字节）。这是在减少香农熵——但代价是计算过程中耗费了能量，向环境排放了热力学熵。

**Upward (compression / abstraction / dimension reduction):** from high-dimensional, redundancy-laden raw signals, extract low-dimensional, compact structural representations. For example, compressing a movie (GBs of pixel arrays) into a one-sentence summary (dozens of bytes). This reduces Shannon entropy — but at the cost of energy consumed during computation, dumping thermodynamic entropy into the environment.

**向下（生成／展开／高维化）**：从低维的紧凑描述出发，展开出高维的丰富结构。比如从"一只猫坐在垫子上"生成一张高分辨率图像。这是在增加特定结构的香农熵——同样以环境中的热力学熵为代价。

**Downward (generation / unfolding / increasing dimension):** from a low-dimensional compact description, unfold rich high-dimensional structures. For instance, generating a high-resolution image from "a cat sitting on a mat." This increases Shannon entropy of specific structures — likewise at the cost of thermodynamic entropy in the environment.

**在尺度楼梯上每爬一步——无论向上还是向下——都必须在热力学上"付账"。** 兰道尔界限和香农源编码定理，分别是物理世界和信息世界中的两个账本。

**Every step on the scale staircase — whether upward or downward — must be "paid for" thermodynamically.** Landauer's bound and Shannon's source coding theorem are the two ledgers of the physical and informational worlds, respectively.

### 压不掉的熵：结构即不可压缩性

Incompressible Entropy: Structure as Incompressibility

一个随机比特串无法被压缩——它的每个比特都必须原样存储，因为没有任何规律可循。它的香农熵等于它的长度。

A random bit string cannot be compressed — every bit must be stored as-is, because there is no pattern to exploit. Its Shannon entropy equals its length.

一段描述银河系公转的微分方程，却可以被压缩到一页纸上，然后在任何时刻展开为完整的轨道预测。它的信息熵远小于它的"展开态"数据量。

A differential equation describing the Milky Way's orbit, however, can be compressed to a single page, then unfolded at any moment into complete orbital predictions. Its information entropy is far smaller than the data volume of its "unfolded state."

因此，**压缩率是一个东西"有多少结构"的客观度量。** 越有结构（规律、对称性、冗余），越可压缩；越无结构（随机、混沌、最大熵），越不可压缩。

Thus, **compression ratio is an objective measure of how much "structure" a thing has.** The more structure (regularities, symmetries, redundancies), the more compressible; the less structure (randomness, chaos, maximum entropy), the less compressible.

这给出了上一章中"数学结构vs物理结构"的一个全新视角：

This provides a new perspective on "math structure vs physics structure" from the previous chapter:

- **数学结构**：可以被压缩到几个公理和推理规则。从这个种子出发，所有定理都能"重新计算出来"。
- **Math structure:** compressible to a few axioms and inference rules. From this seed, all theorems can be "recomputed."

- **物理结构**：需要被逐点观测。你的终极压缩版本受限于你对宇宙初始条件和演化规则的了解——而这些规则本身，就是物理学家在寻找的那个"最优压缩程序"。
- **Physics structure:** must be observed point by point. Your ultimate compressed version is bounded by your knowledge of the universe's initial conditions and evolutionary rules — and these rules themselves are precisely that "optimal compression program" that physicists seek.

数学和物理，在这个视角下，不过是一个巨大的压缩金字塔的顶端和底端。计算科学，就是在这座金字塔里爬上爬下。

Math and physics, from this perspective, are but the top and the bottom of a giant compression pyramid. Computing science is the business of climbing up and down within this pyramid.

### 本章小结：从公理到现实

Chapter Summary: from Axiom to Reality

1. 信息是物理的。比特必须寄居在物质之上。
Information is physical. A bit must reside on matter.

2. 热力学熵和信息熵共享同一个数学核心：$$-k \sum p \log p$$。前者在原子尺度，后者在符号尺度——但它们是一样东西。
Thermodynamic entropy and information entropy share the same mathematical core: -k Σ p log p. The former at the atomic scale, the latter at the symbolic scale — but they are the same thing.

3. 计算是有热力学代价的。兰道尔界限（kT ln 2 / bit）是物理宇宙对信息处理征税的最低税率。
Computation has a thermodynamic cost. Landauer's bound (kT ln 2 / bit) is the physical universe's minimum tax rate on information processing.

4. 可压缩性 = 结构。一个系统的信息熵与它的最大可压缩长度之间的差距，就是那个系统所包含的"可被理解的部分"。
Compressibility = structure. The gap between a system's information entropy and its maximum compressible length is the "understandable part" contained in that system.

5. 计算科学发展出的全部技术——从数据结构到压缩算法到机器学习——最终都可以理解为：**在热力学代价的约束下，高效地完成信息熵的转换。**
All the techniques developed by computing science — from data structures to compression algorithms to machine learning — can ultimately be understood as: **efficiently completing the conversion of information entropy, under the constraint of thermodynamic cost.**

### 附：流形假说——几何版本的"结构即压缩"

Appendix: The Manifold Hypothesis — a geometric version of "structure equals compression"

（本节供学生自行探索。它尚未被严格证明为普遍定理，但已深刻塑造了现代机器学习的实践。）
(This section is for independent exploration. It has not been rigorously proven as a universal theorem, but has profoundly shaped the practice of modern machine learning.)

考虑一个自然图像数据集。每张图像是 256×256 像素，每个像素3个颜色通道。在"所有可能的像素组合"这个空间里，一张图的自由度是 256 × 256 × 3 ≈ 200,000 维。

Consider a dataset of natural images. Each image is 256×256 pixels, 3 color channels per pixel. In the space of "all possible pixel combinations," one image has 256 × 256 × 3 ≈ 200,000 degrees of freedom.

但如果你随机采样这200,000维空间中的向量，你看到的不是猫、人脸或风景——你看到的是电视雪花（白噪声）。实际上，**所有你见过的、叫得出名字的自然图像，加在一起，在这200,000维空间中占据的体积接近于零。**

But if you randomly sample vectors from this 200,000-dimensional space, what you see is not cats, faces, or landscapes — you see TV static (white noise). In fact, **all natural images you have ever seen that you can name, combined, occupy a volume in this 200,000-dimensional space that is nearly zero.**

流形假说（Manifold Hypothesis）断言：**现实世界中的高维数据，实际上分布在一个远低于环境维度的低维流形之上（或附近）。**

The Manifold Hypothesis asserts: **real-world high-dimensional data actually distributes on (or near) a low-dimensional manifold whose intrinsic dimension is far lower than the ambient dimension.**

以人脸照片为例。一张人脸的变化由少数几个"真正"的变量控制：光照方向（2维）、头部朝向（3维）、表情（几个维度）、身份（几十到几百维）。你在像素空间里看到的20万维变化，大部分是这些低维变量在像素空间中的"投影冗余"。

Take face photos as an example. The variations in a face are controlled by a small number of "true" variables: lighting direction (2D), head pose (3D), expression (a few dimensions), identity (tens to hundreds of dimensions). Most of the 200,000-dimensional variation you observe in pixel space is the "projection redundancy" of these low-dimensional variables in pixel space.

几百个真实自由度，铺展在20万维的环境中。压缩率：约 1000:1。
A few hundred true degrees of freedom, spread across 200,000 ambient dimensions. Compression ratio: roughly 1000:1.

这恰好是「结构即不可压缩性」一节的几何表述：**数据集内部那个低维流形，就是它的"结构"；流形的维度，就是它的"不可压缩熵"。** 找到这个流形，就是完成了我们在「熵作为尺度放缩的度量」一节所说的"向上爬"——从高维的像素冗余中，提取出低维的语义结构。

This is exactly the geometric formulation of "structure as compression" from the「结构即不可压缩性」section: **the low-dimensional manifold inside the dataset is its "structure"; the dimension of that manifold is its "incompressible entropy."** Finding this manifold is completing the "upward climb" we described in the「熵作为尺度放缩的度量」section — extracting low-dimensional semantic structure from high-dimensional pixel redundancy.

从信息论的角度看，流形假说提供了一个更直观的画面：

From an information-theoretic perspective, the Manifold Hypothesis offers a more intuitive picture:

- 高维环境空间 = 可能的消息全集（最大香农熵）
- High-dimensional ambient space = the universe of all possible messages (maximum Shannon entropy)

- 低维流形 = 有意义的、有结构的消息子集（真正的条件熵）
- Low-dimensional manifold = the subset of meaningful, structured messages (the true conditional entropy)

- 流形学习（Manifold Learning）和深度学习 = 从数据中自动发现这个流形的计算技术
- Manifold Learning and Deep Learning = computational techniques that automatically discover this manifold from data

这也解释了为什么深度神经网络有效。如果数据确实是低维流形的高维嵌入，那么一个神经网络的任务就不是"记忆200,000维空间中的所有猫图片"，而是**学习从少数几个潜在变量（latent variables）到高维像素空间的映射函数**——以及它的逆映射（从像素回到潜在变量）。前者是生成（「熵作为尺度放缩的度量」一节中的"向下爬"），后者是识别／分类／理解（同一节中的"向上爬"）。

This also explains why deep neural networks work. If data is indeed a low-dimensional manifold embedded in a high-dimensional space, then a neural network's task is not "memorizing all cat images in a 200,000-dimensional space," but **learning the mapping function from a few latent variables to the high-dimensional pixel space** — and its inverse (from pixels back to latent variables). The former is generation ("climbing down" in the「熵作为尺度放缩的度量」section), the latter is recognition / classification / understanding ("climbing up" in the same section).

**流形假说，是"尺度放缩"在几何上的终极版本：从高维到低维不是粗暴地扔掉信息，而是找到数据原本就躺在上面睡觉的那张低维床。**

**The Manifold Hypothesis is the ultimate geometric version of "scale zooming": going from high dimension to low dimension is not about brutally discarding information, but about finding the low-dimensional bed on which the data was already sleeping.**
