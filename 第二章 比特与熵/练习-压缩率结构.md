# 练习 3 — 压缩率作为"结构"的度量

Exercise 3 — Compression Ratio as a Measure of "Structure"

以下是一段 10,000 个字符的英文文本（原始字符数为 N₀ = 80,000 比特，按每字符8比特的ASCII编码计）：

"The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog." 重复 200 次。

Below is a 10,000-character English text (raw character count N₀ = 80,000 bits, assuming 8-bit ASCII encoding per character):

"The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog." repeated 200 times.

---

**(a)** 用你能想到的最简方式描述这段文本的内容（但不逐字写出全量文本）。你的描述用了多少比特？你的描述是否构成"压缩"？近似压缩率是多少？

**(a)** Describe the content of this text in the most concise way you can (without writing out the full text verbatim). How many bits did your description use? Does your description constitute "compression"? What is the approximate compression ratio?

---

**(b)** 换一段文本：10,000 个字符的真随机比特（0/1各50%概率）的十六进制表示。你在 (a) 中使用的方法对这种文本有效吗？为什么有效或无效？

**(b)** Now consider a different text: a hexadecimal representation of 10,000 characters' worth of truly random bits (0/1, 50% each). Does the method you used in (a) work for this text? Why or why not?

---

**(c)** 「结构即不可压缩性」一节断言"压缩率是一个东西有多少结构的客观度量"。用你在 (a) 和 (b) 中的发现，论证或反驳这个断言。

**(c)** The「结构即不可压缩性」section asserts that "compression ratio is an objective measure of how much structure a thing has." Using your findings from (a) and (b), defend or refute this assertion.
