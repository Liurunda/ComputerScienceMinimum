# 练习 5 — 为你的手机相册估算流形维度

Exercise 5 — Estimate the Manifold Dimension of Your Phone's Photo Gallery

打开你的手机相册，随机浏览 100 张照片。（无需拿出手机，在脑中完成估算即可。）

Open your phone's photo gallery and browse 100 random photos. (You can do this estimation in your head.)

---

**(a)** 每张照片的分辨率是多少像素？每个像素几个颜色通道？你浏览的这100张照片在"原始像素空间"中的环境维度是多少？

**(a)** What is the resolution of each photo in pixels? How many color channels per pixel? What is the ambient dimension of these 100 photos in the "raw pixel space"?

---

**(b)** 如果你只能用少数几个"真实变量"来描述这100张照片之间的变化，你会选哪些变量？大约需要多少个变量才能覆盖大部分有意义的差异？

**(b)** If you could describe the variations among these 100 photos using only a small number of "true variables," which variables would you choose? Roughly how many variables would be needed to cover most meaningful differences?

*提示：照片在什么地方拍的？拍了什么人／物？时间段？光线条件？*

*Hint: where were photos taken? what/who was photographed? time of day? lighting conditions?*

---

**(c)** 估算你相册的"流形维度"与环境维度的压缩比。这个数字与你未来可以使用最好的压缩算法把照片文件夹压缩到的最小体积之间，有什么理论关系？

**(c)** Estimate the compression ratio between your photo gallery's "manifold dimension" and its ambient dimension. What is the theoretical relationship between this number and the smallest possible file size you could achieve by compressing your photo folder with the best possible compression algorithm?
