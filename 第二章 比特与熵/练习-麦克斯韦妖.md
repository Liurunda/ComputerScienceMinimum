# 练习 4 — 麦克斯韦妖的账本

Exercise 4 — Maxwell's Demon's Ledger

回顾「麦克斯韦妖与兰道尔界限」一节的麦克斯韦妖。妖在一个充满气体分子的容器中工作。假设：
- 容器中有 N 个分子
- 妖每次测量一个分子的速度，以1比特记录"快/慢"
- 妖的存储容量为 M 比特（M ≪ N）

Review Maxwell's demon from the「麦克斯韦妖与兰道尔界限」section. The demon operates in a container filled with gas molecules. Assume:
- There are N molecules in the container
- The demon measures each molecule's speed and records "fast/slow" as 1 bit
- The demon's storage capacity is M bits (M ≪ N)

---

**(a)** 在妖测量了 M 个分子之后，它的存储器已满。接下来它必须做什么才能继续工作？这个动作为什么会增加宇宙的总熵？

**(a)** After the demon has measured M molecules, its storage is full. What must it do next to continue working? Why does this action increase the total entropy of the universe?

---

**(b)** 如果妖的存储器是无限大的（M → ∞），它是否能永久降低容器内的热力学熵而不付代价？请论证你的答案——这个问题的答案正是物理学家花了近百年才想明白的关键。

**(b)** If the demon's storage were infinitely large (M → ∞), could it permanently decrease the thermodynamic entropy inside the container without paying any cost? Justify your answer — this is precisely the key point that took physicists nearly a century to figure out.

---

**(c)** （开放题）你刚刚分析的过程——"测量→存储→在存储满时擦除以腾出空间"——与你日常使用计算机时的哪些操作在结构上同构？举一个具体场景。

**(c)** (Open) The process you just analyzed — "measure → store → erase when full to make room" — is structurally isomorphic to which operations in your daily computer use? Give one concrete scenario.
