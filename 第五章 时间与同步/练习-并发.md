# 练习 2 — 并发与并行：时间维度的尺度放大

Exercise 2 — Concurrency and Parallelism: Scaling Up the Temporal Dimension

本章讨论了一个 CPU 内部如何用时钟信号维持"绝对现在"，以及跨出芯片之后如何用 Lamport 逻辑时钟替代全局时序。并发与并行是同一个主题在不同尺度上的延伸——当多个任务同时在时间轴上展开时，它们如何不互相踩踏对方的脚？

This chapter discussed how a clock signal maintains an "absolute now" inside a CPU, and how Lamport logical clocks replace global timing once you step outside the chip. Concurrency and parallelism extend the same topic across different scales — when multiple tasks unfold simultaneously on the timeline, how do they avoid stepping on each other's toes?

---

**(1)** 两个线程同时执行 `x = x + 1`（x 初始为 0）。各执行 1000 次。最终 x 可能小于 2000。为什么？用本章的时间概念解释：两条指令的"先后顺序"在共享内存中并没有被**全局时钟**定义——每个核有自己的本地"现在"。

Two threads simultaneously execute `x = x + 1` (x initially 0), each 1000 times. The final x may be less than 2000. Why? Use this chapter's time concepts to explain: the "before/after" ordering of two instructions is not defined by a **global clock** in shared memory — each core has its own local "now."

---

**(2)** Amdahl 定律说：一个程序的加速比受限于它**不可并行化的部分**所占的比例 s。如果 s = 10%，即使你有无穷多个核，加速比也不可能超过 10 倍。用第二章的语言重述这句结论：**程序的"不可并行部分"是什么的度量？**

Amdahl's Law states: a program's speedup is limited by the fraction s of the program that **cannot be parallelized**. If s = 10%, even with infinitely many cores, speedup cannot exceed 10×. Restate this conclusion in the language of Chapter 2: **the "non-parallelizable fraction" is a measure of what?**

*提示：系统某部分的依赖结构——每一步确实需要前面那一步的——结果。Hint: the dependency structure — where each step genuinely requires the result of the previous step — within the system.*

---

**(3)** Actor 模型的一种简要描述：每个 Actor 是一个独立的计算单元，有自己私有的状态和邮箱。Actor 之间通过**异步消息**通信——没有共享内存，没有全局锁。当一个 Actor 收到一个消息时，它在自己的时间轴内处理它、更新自己的私有状态、然后向下一个收件人发消息。

One brief description of the Actor model: each Actor is an independent computation unit with its own private state and mailbox. Actors communicate through **asynchronous messages** — no shared memory, no global locks. When an Actor receives a message, it processes it within its own timeline, updates its private state, and sends a message to the next recipient.

这和第五章的"互联网尺度——连因果偏序都扔掉了"形成了什么对应？在 Actor 模型的并发系统中，两个 Actor 之间的"时序"以什么形式存在？

How does this correspond to Chapter 5's "Internet scale — even causal partial order is discarded"? In an Actor-model concurrent system, in what form does "timing" between two Actors exist?
