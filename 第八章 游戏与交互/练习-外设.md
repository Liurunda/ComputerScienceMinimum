# 练习 4 — 用键盘和鼠标之外的方式工作

Exercise 4 — Working Without a Keyboard and Mouse

本章描述了人机交互的历史弧线——但这条弧线至今主要围绕"键鼠 + 屏幕"展开。键盘和鼠标不是和计算机交互的唯一通道。

This chapter traced the historical arc of HCI — but that arc has so far largely revolved around "keyboard + mouse + screen." Keyboards and mice are not the only channels for interacting with a computer.

---

**(1)** 键盘和鼠标为什么成为了默认？它们解决了什么问题，又制造了什么限制？

Why did keyboards and mice become the default? What problems did they solve, and what limitations did they create?

*提示：打字速度受限于手指移动精确度；鼠标精度受限于桌面空间和手腕角度；两者都要求用户静止地坐着。Hint: typing speed is limited by finger movement precision; mouse accuracy is limited by desk space and wrist angle; both require the user to sit still.*

---

**(2)** 设想你用**手柄 + 语音输入**完全替代键鼠，在你的电脑上工作一小时。手柄负责光标移动和点击，语音负责文字输入。你会遇到哪些困难？有哪些事反而比键鼠更舒适？

Imagine replacing your keyboard and mouse entirely with a **gamepad + voice input** for one hour of work on your computer. The gamepad handles cursor movement and clicking; voice handles text input. What difficulties would you encounter? What tasks would actually be more comfortable than with keyboard/mouse?

*提示：写代码靠语音？操作终端靠手柄？Hint: coding by voice? navigating the terminal with a gamepad?*

---

**(3)** 讨论以下每个替代方案：它最可能在哪一类任务上优于键鼠？最不可能在哪一类任务上替代键鼠？

For each of the following alternatives: on what type of task is it most likely to outperform keyboard/mouse? On what type of task is it least likely to replace them?

- 数位板 + 触控笔 Drawing tablet + stylus
- 眼动追踪 + 脚踏板 Eye tracker + foot pedals
- 手势识别摄像头（如 Leap Motion）Hand gesture recognition camera (e.g., Leap Motion)
- 基于视差的3D视觉（头戴显示，或基于柱状透镜阵列和视线追踪的裸眼3D）Parallax-based 3D vision (head-mounted display, or glasses-free 3D based on lenticular lens array and eye tracking)

---

**(4)** 提出你自己的方案——任何你可以想象到的硬件外设（或外设组合）——用它们来代替键鼠操作电脑。它不需要"现在就实用"，但必须描述清楚：输入通道、反馈通道，以及它最适合的特定任务或用户群体。

Propose your own scheme — any hardware peripheral (or combination of peripherals) you can imagine — to replace keyboard/mouse for operating a computer. It does not need to be "practical right now," but you must clearly describe: input channel, feedback channel, and the specific task or user group it is best suited for.

*提示：可以是为了某种特殊场景（水下？太空？），也可以是来自另一个领域（乐器？健身器械？）。Hint: it could be for a special environment (underwater? space?) or borrowed from another domain (musical instruments? fitness equipment?).*
